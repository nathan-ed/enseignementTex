	&=\displaystyle\lim_{8x \to 0} \dfrac{\sin(8x)}{8x}\cdot \dfrac{8}{10}\\
	&=\dfrac{4}{5}
		\end{aligned}$

&=\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 2} \dfrac{\sin(2x-4)}{4(2x-4)}\\
&=\displaystyle\lim_{2x-4 \to 0} \dfrac{\sin(2x-4)}{2x-4}\cdot\dfrac{1}{4}\\
&=\dfrac{1}{4}
\end{aligned}

\end{aligned}$
